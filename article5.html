With T-Cops Finance Locker, retailers can conveniently monitor the timing and dates of EMI payments made by customers. This feature provides valuable insights into payment patterns and helps identify any potential issues or delays.

3.Planned EMI Payments:

Customers are required to make EMI payments on a specified day every month, ensuring consistency and predictability in payment schedules. This helps both retailers and customers plan their finances more effectively.

The user-friendly payment platform offered by T-Cops Finance Locker allows customers to make EMI transactions conveniently. Whether it's through cash payments or scanning a QR code on their phone's lock screen, customers have multiple options to complete their payments hassle-free.
