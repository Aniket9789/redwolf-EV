OUR JOURNEY

RedWolf Hi-T ech embarked on its journey in 2018 with a bold vision to disrupt the tech industry. Led by Rohit Jain, Chirag Sachdeva, and Pankaj Chaturvedi, the company quickly gained recognition for its innovative approach and commitment to excellence. In the same year, we introduced our flagship product, Finance Locker, marking the beginning of a transformative era in financial management solutions.

Finance locker

Finance Locker, our pioneering product launched in 2019, redefined the way retailers manage their finances. With models such as T-Cops Zt Pro, T-Cops Zt Plus, and T-Cops I Mac, Finance Locker empowered retailers to efficiently oversee their financed customers and streamline the payment process. In the event of delayed EMI payments, T-Cops I Mac provided a seamless solution, ensuring smooth transactions and enhanced customer satisfaction.

RedWolf EV:

In 2024, RedWolf Hi-Tech expanded its portfolio with the launch of RedWolf EV, an electric vehicle aimed at revolutionizing urban mobility. With a focus on sustainability and innovation, RedWolf EV offers eco-friendly transportation solutions that are both efficient and reliable. Designed for urban commuters, RedWolf EV scooters combine cutting-edge technology with sleek design, offering a smooth and enjoyable riding experience. With RedWolf EV, we are committed to reducing carbon emissions and building a greener future for generations to come.


IT Solutions:

Leveraging our expertise in technology, RedWolf Hi-Tech offers comprehensive IT solutions to businesses of all sizes. From website development and app development to graphic design and digital marketing, our team of skilled professionals delivers customized solutions that drive engagement, increase visibility, and drive results. With a focus on innovation and creativity, we help our clients harness the power of technology to achieve their goals and stay ahead in today's digital landscape.

Skribbit:

In 2024, RedWolf Hi-Tech introduced Skribbit, a pioneering e-commerce platform tailored to cater to business-to-business retailers and corporate stationery needs. With an extensive range of products including colorful notebooks, pens, and all essential stationery items, Skribbit aims to streamline the procurement process for businesses. By providing a one-stop solution for stationery supplies, Skribbit endeavors to inspire creativity and efficiency in the workplace while simplifying the purchasing experience for corporate clients.
